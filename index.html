Welcome to VRPS

Vaddera Rastra Porata Samithi

Vaddera Rastra Porata Samithi (VRPS) is a dedicated organization that strives to uplift and empower the Vaddera community, while also fostering unity and progress across the nation. With a rich history and a commitment to social justice, VRPS works tirelessly to address the challenges faced by the Vaddera community and promote their welfare.
